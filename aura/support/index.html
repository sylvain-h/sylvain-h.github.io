Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to any server. There are no accounts and no cloud sync.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export data. You can export your migraines and check-ins as CSV files to share with your doctor or open in a spreadsheet.

What happens if I delete the app?

All your data is permanently deleted. There is no way to recover it since nothing is stored outside your device. Consider exporting your data first.

Why does aura ask for my location?

Only to fetch current weather conditions (barometric pressure, temperature) when you log an entry. This helps identify weather-related patterns. Location access is optional — the app works fully without it.

When do patterns appear?

After you've logged at least 5 migraines with some daily check-ins. The more data you provide, the more accurate the observations become.

Can I edit or delete an entry?

Yes. Tap any migraine entry to view its details, then use Edit to modify it or Delete to remove it.
