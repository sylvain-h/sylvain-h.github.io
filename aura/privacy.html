Privacy Policy

aura — migraine companion · Last updated March 29, 2026

The short version

aura does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your iPhone.

Data storage

All data you enter in aura — migraine entries, daily check-ins, medications, notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, no servers, and no databases controlled by us. If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted.

Location data

aura may request your location when you log a migraine or daily check-in. This is used solely to fetch current weather conditions (barometric pressure, temperature, humidity) through Apple's WeatherKit service, which may help identify weather-related migraine patterns.

  • Location is only accessed at the moment you log an entry — never in the background
  • Your location is sent to Apple's WeatherKit service to retrieve weather data, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Your location is not stored by aura — only the resulting weather data (pressure, temperature, humidity) is saved locally
  • Location access is entirely optional — the app works fully without it

Analytics and tracking

aura contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not know who uses the app or how it is used.

Third-party services

The only external service used by aura is Apple WeatherKit, which is operated by Apple Inc. and subject to their privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Data export

You can export your data as CSV files at any time through the app's Settings. Exported files are generated locally on your device. What you do with those files is entirely your choice.

Children's privacy

aura does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is transmitted from the app, no special provisions are required.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since aura does not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at privacy@aura-app.com.
